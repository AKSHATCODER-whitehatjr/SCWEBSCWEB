What's New

  • School App

    When your child/children comes home, you will probably ask them, "What did you do at school today?" Even though your child/children would tell you what he/she did at school that day, this app will make it easier to find out. Each child has an account, just type their name on the app, and then pictures and some text about what they did at school will pop up. Now, you don't even have to ask, and what happens at school appears on the app at around the same time, so you can check, even when your child/children are at class. If there is an event, it will appear on the school calender, on the home menu

  • Online Food Ordering

    If you don't have the time to pack your kids lunch, you can order it online, which will then serve your child for a month, depending on the different foods you order for each day of the week. You have many foods to choose, with a side, like apple sauce, fruit of the week, and more. After a month, you will have to order again, so you can change the food if you want, or you can keep the food.
